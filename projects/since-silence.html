N°001 · Feature · 2025

Since Silence

A woman returns to the town she outran. An aching slow-burn about what a voice costs, and what gets kept quiet so a family can keep eating.

Role

Meera — lead. A translator who hasn't spoken her mother tongue in fifteen years, called home for a funeral she's not sure is hers to attend.

Synopsis

Set between Chennai and a small coastal town, Since Silence follows Meera as she unpacks not just the house she grew up in but the decade of emails she never sent. Director Arun Sethuraman's second feature — spare, patient, and quietly devastating — premiered at the MAMI Film Festival in late 2025.

Press

"A performance built on restraint. Muthyam gives her character the kind of interior life you usually only see in novels." — The Hindu
