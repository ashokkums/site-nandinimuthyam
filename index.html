I have always been drawn to what exists beneath words. The pause before a response, the flicker in a gaze that gives something away. Trained in Koothuppattarai and Bharatanatyam, I found my language in the space between. As an actor, I am not interested in performing emotion as much as allowing it to surface. My work lives where the body remembers, silence is not empty, and the unspoken tells the fuller story. This is me, in motion.
