About

I tell stories — on stage, on screen, and in the space between a breath and a step.

Trained in Bharatanatyam from the age of six, I've spent the last decade finding the places where classical dance, contemporary theater, and independent film overlap.

Background

Based in Austin, Texas. Tamil by heritage, American by birth, storyteller by training.

Disciplines

Dance — Bharatanatyam (primary), Kuchipudi, folk forms.
Acting — stage (primary), on-camera, voice work.
Filmmaking — writing and directing short films.

Currently

Writing a short film. Rehearsing for a spring dance recital. Open to collaborations — see the contact page.
