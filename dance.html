Arushya

A school rooted in the Kalakshetra tradition — where classical form meets living practice, and every student finds their own beginning.

Founder and Principal of Arushya School of Dance — Kalakshetra-style Bharatanatyam, founded in Chennai in 2003, now based in Austin & Round Rock, TX. Trained under Shri Adayar K. Lakshman and Smt. Kalanidhi Narayanan. Teaching children and adults across all levels.
